# Cluster Headache Research Collection

## Toxicology

- WHO critical review of 5-MeO-DALT pharmacology, toxicology, and dependence potential [@who2020-5meodalt].
  - No direct evidence of 5-MeO-DALT causing adverse effects on its own.
- Toxicology and pharmacological interactions of classic psychedelics [@thomas2024-toxicology]
  - No death directly attributable to DMT
- Reconsidering evidence for psychedelics-induced psychosis [@sabe2024-psychosis]
  - Classic psychedelics are unlikely to induce psychosis overall, and in particular in patients with schizophrenia (which are thought to be more at risk)

## Preparation and administration

### DMT

- Continuous flow synthesis of DMT analogues (including 5-MeO-DMT) with therapeutic potential for cluster headache and depression [@simoens2025-dmt-flow].
  - DMT can easily oxidize: "in order to prevent oxidation towards the respective N-oxides, a known degradation product of these compounds"

## Efficacy

### Triptans

- Sumatriptan (Imitrex) subcutaneous (SC) injection prescription info sheet [@sumatriptan-subcutaneous-injection]
  - With a  6mg SC injection: Average time til peak blood concentration following SC injection is 7 min, and relief occurs within 1 min of that. By 15 minutes, ~75% of the patients report relief. 

### Oxygen

- Oxygen onset time [@oxygen-onset]
  - **Very roughly**, 90% of the patients who find relief from high-flow oxygen find it withing 5-20 minutes of starting using oxygen.
